# 📦 Amazon Leadership Principle (LP) Matrix

Amazon uses these 16 LPs to grade candidates in *every* round. Use this matrix to map your 12 core stories (S01-S12) to the LPs.

---

## 🗺️ LP-to-Story Mapping

| Leadership Principle | Recommended Story | Key Signal to Prove |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Customer Obsession** | S01, S10 | I start with the customer and work backwards. |
| **Ownership** | S05, S09 | I never say "that's not my job." |
| **Invent and Simplify** | S02, S06 | I find simple solutions to complex problems. |
| **Are Right, A Lot** | S09, S11 | I have strong judgment and seek diverse perspectives. |
| **Learn and Be Curious** | S06, S03 | I am never done learning. |
| **Hire and Develop the Best** | S12 | I help others grow. |
| **Insist on Highest Standards** | S08, S02 | I don't compromise on quality. |
| **Think Big** | S01, S02 | I look for bold directions. |
| **Bias for Action** | S05, S07 | Speed matters in business. |
| **Frugality** | S01, S11 | I achieve more with less. |
| **Earn Trust** | S03, S04 | I am vocally self-critical. |
| **Dive Deep** | S02, S11 | I stay connected to the details. |
| **Disagree and Commit** | S04, S09 | I challenge decisions respectfully, then commit. |
| **Deliver Results** | S01, S08 | I overcome obstacles to meet deadlines. |

---

## 💡 Pro-Tip for Amazon Rounds
If the interviewer asks: *"Tell me more about that,"* they are testing your **Dive Deep**. Be ready with the metrics, the trade-offs you considered, and the technical implementation details.
