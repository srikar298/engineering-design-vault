### Step 2 — Speak It (10 min)
1. **Route to your story** → [STORY_QUESTION_MAP.md](file:///e:/job-hunt/LLD/LLD-Design-Patterns-main/behavioral/questions/STORY_QUESTION_MAP.md)

### Step 3 — Self-Audit (5 min)
Listen back to the first 30 seconds. Ask:
- [ ] Did I open with a sharp Situation sentence?
- [ ] Did I say a metric in the Result?
- [ ] Was I under 120 seconds?
- [ ] Did I use "um/uh" more than 3 times?

## 📋 Quick Daily Log (6 Columns — Fill This, Not the 26-Column Sheet)

Copy this row template into your Google Sheet **Sheet: Quick_Log**:

```
Date | Story_Used | Question_Category | Duration_Sec | Filler_Count | Hire_Signal
```

**Hire Signal**: `Y` = felt confident, structured, specific. `N` = vague, rushed, or no metric.

### 📋 Google Sheets Auto Hire-Rate Formula (weekly trend cell):
```excel
=TEXT(COUNTIF(Hire_Signal_Column,"Y")/COUNTA(Hire_Signal_Column),"0%") & " hire signal rate this week"
```
