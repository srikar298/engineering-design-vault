# 🏢 Company Intel & Value Alignment

Before any interview, use this template to research the company and align your "Values" with their "Principles."

---

## 1. The Research Checklist
- [ ] What is their Mission Statement?
- [ ] What was their most recent major technical public disclosure (e.g., Engineering Blog post)?
- [ ] What are their 3 core products/revenue drivers?
- [ ] What is their engineering culture known for? (e.g., Google = Curiosity/Scale, Amazon = LPs/Customer, Meta = Speed).

## 3. High-Signal Questions for Interviewers

Use these to show you think like a Senior Engineer:
1.  *"How does the team handle the trade-off between shipping features and managing technical debt?"*
2.  *"What does the growth path for an Individual Contributor (IC) look like at this company versus a management path?"*
3.  *"Can you tell me about a time the team had to pivot after shipping something and how you handled that?"*
4.  *"How is engineering excellence measured here? What separates a 'Good' engineer from a 'Great' one in your eyes?"*
