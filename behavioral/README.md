---

## 🧠 Philosophy: Strategic Storytelling

Behavioral interviews are not about "answering questions." they are about **providing evidence** of:
1.  **Complexity Management**: Can you handle messy, ambiguous situations?
2.  **Engineering Judgment**: Do you understand trade-offs beyond just code?
3.  **Leadership & Ownership**: Do you make the team better, even without a title?
4.  **Growth Mindset**: How do you respond when things go wrong?

## 🚀 How to use this guide

1.  **Check the Sheet**: Open your `STORY BANK` sheet to see which story needs drafting or rehearsal.
2.  **Draft in the Vault**: Use the [Story Templates](./STORY_BANK_TEMPLATES.md) to structure your answer using **STAR+ (Situation, Task, Action, Result + Reflection)**.
3.  **Rehearse Aloud**: Use the [Mock Prep Guide](./MOCK_PREP_GUIDE.md) to eliminate filler words and hit the 2-minute mark.
4.  **Log Diagnostics**: Record your `Confidence_Score` and `Filler_Count` in your Google Sheet after every rehearsal.
