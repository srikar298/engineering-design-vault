# 🪞 Behavioral Foundations: Self-Discovery Dojo

Before you can tell a great story, you must mine your career for high-signal moments. Use these inventories to gather the "raw material" for your **Story Bank**.

---

## 🏗️ 1. Achievement Inventory (The Metrics Mine)
*Goal: Find the data for S01 (Achievement) and S02 (Innovation).*

| Project/Period | What was the "Before"? | What was the "After"? | The "Hero Metric" |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| | (e.g., 800ms latency) | (e.g., 120ms latency) | **85% improvement** |
| | (e.g., 10 manual steps) | (e.g., fully automated) | **Saved 40 hrs/week** |

---

## ❌ 2. Failure & Growth Log
*Goal: Find the authenticity for S03 (Failure) and S09 (Decisions).*

*   **The Moment:** "I underestimated the migration time by 3 weeks."
*   **The Honest Mistake:** "I didn't account for data inconsistencies in legacy records."
*   **The "Senior" Learning:** "I learned to always 'Spike' before estimating complex legacy work."
*   **Growth Since:** "In the next project, I insisted on a 2-day discovery phase, and we hit the deadline exactly."

---

## ⚔️ 3. Conflict & Resolution Tracker
*Goal: Find the maturity for S04 (Conflict) and S10 (Cross-team).*

*   **Technical Disagreement:** "A teammate wanted to use MongoDB for a highly relational payment ledger."
*   **My Position:** "I advocated for Postgres to ensure ACID guarantees for financial data."
*   **How it was Resolved:** "I built a small benchmark prototype showing the risk of data drift in Mongo. We agreed and committed to Postgres."

---

## 🚀 4. Leadership Moment Log
*Goal: Find the signal for S05 (Leadership w/o Authority) and S12 (Mentoring).*

*   **Mentorship:** "I coached a Junior dev on distributed tracing. They now lead the observability guild."
*   **Initiative:** "I noticed we lacked a standard PR checklist. I created one, and production bugs dropped by 12%."
*   **Ownership:** "The on-call rotation was burning people out. I proposed a 'Follow-the-Sun' model to my manager."

---

## 💎 5. My Engineering Identity
*Define these once to ground your elevator pitch:*
*   **My Superpower:** [e.g., "Modularizing legacy monoliths without downtime"]
*   **My Philosophy:** [e.g., "Observability is not optional; it's the foundation of production confidence."]
*   **My Biggest Weakness:** [e.g., "I sometimes dive too deep into technical perfection at the cost of shipping speed—I now use MVP time-boxing to manage this."]
