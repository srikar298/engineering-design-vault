# 🎭 The 12 Must-Have Story Templates (STAR+)

Use these templates to draft your core 12 stories. Your goal is to move from **📝 Draft** to **🏆 Mastered** in your Tracking Sheet.

---

## 🏆 S01: Biggest Achievement (C001)
**Primary Question:** "Tell me about your greatest professional accomplishment."
**The Senior Signal:** Impact at scale and extreme ownership.

*   **S:** Set the context. 10M users? Legacy monolith?
*   **T:** What was *your* specific responsibility?
*   **A:** What 3-4 specific steps did you take? Explain the *Why*.
*   **R:** Use the **Hero Metric** (e.g., "Reduced silent failures by 98%").
*   **+:** What does this achievement prove about your engineering judgment?

---

## 💡 S02: Technical Innovation (C002)
**Primary Question:** "Tell me about a complex technical problem you solved."
**The Senior Signal:** Systematic thinking and trade-off mastery.

*   **S:** A problem that didn't have a Google-able answer.
*   **A:** Explain the 2-3 alternatives you rejected before choosing your path.
*   **R:** Performance, Cost, or Developer Experience improvement.

---

## ❌ S03: Failure & Recovery (C003)
**Primary Question:** "Tell me about a time you failed or made a mistake."
**The Senior Signal:** Intellectual honesty and zero blame.

*   **S:** A genuine failure (not a "disguised strength").
*   **A:** How did you take ownership? How did you communicate it?
*   **R:** The **Post-Mortem** outcome and the mechanism you built to prevent it.

---

## ⚔️ S04: Conflict Resolution (C004)
**Primary Question:** "Tell me about a disagreement with a teammate or manager."
**The Senior Signal:** Professional maturity and data-driven debate.

*   **A:** How did you seek to understand their perspective first?
*   **+:** How did you "Disagree and Commit" after the decision was made?

---

## 🚀 S05: Leadership Without Authority (C005)
**Primary Question:** "Tell me when you led without being the lead."
**The Senior Signal:** Influence and proactive initiative.

---

## 🌪️ S07: Handling Ambiguity (C007)
**Primary Question:** "Tell me about a time you had to work with unclear requirements."
**The Senior Signal:** Independence and proactive clarification.

*   **A:** How did you break the problem down? What clarifying questions did you ask?

---

## 💔 S09: Unpopular Decision (C009)
**Primary Question:** "Tell me about an unpopular decision you made."
**The Senior Signal:** Conviction based on data.

---

## 🌱 S12: Mentoring & Growing Others (C012)
**Primary Question:** "Tell me about a time you helped someone grow."
**The Senior Signal:** Leadership maturity and generosity.

---

## 🕵️ The "Depth Test" Follow-up Bank
*Expect these for every story:*
1. "What specifically did you do in step 2?" (Testing for "I" vs "We").
2. "What were the 2 other alternatives you considered?" (Testing for Judgment).
3. "What would you change if you did this again today?" (Testing for Reflection).
4. "How did you measure the success of that?" (Testing for Data-literacy).
5. "How did the team react to your proposal?" (Testing for Soft Skills).
