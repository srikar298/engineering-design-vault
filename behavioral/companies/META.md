# 🏢 Company Profile: Meta

Meta's engineering culture is centered around **speed**, **execution**, and **direct business impact**. Their loops evaluate your ability to build fast, take calculated risks, and solve scale issues.

---

## 💎 Meta's Core Engineering Values

### 1. Move Fast
*   **Signal**: High execution velocity, fast feedback loops, incremental ship-cycles, and building MVPs to validate designs.
*   **Key Question**: *"Tell me about your most impactful project. How quickly did you get the first version to production?"*

### 2. Focus on Impact
*   **Signal**: Moving core business metrics (engagement, performance latency, cost savings).
*   **Key Question**: *"Tell me about a time you chose a simple solution over a complex architecture to hit a business goal."*

### 3. Be Bold
*   **Signal**: Taking calculated technical risks, pushing boundaries, refactoring critical legacy paths.
*   **Key Question**: *"Tell me about a time you took a significant technical risk that paid off."*

### 4. Build Awesome Things
*   **Signal**: Passion for product craftsmanship and solving hard engineering problems at scale.
*   **Key Question**: *"What is the most technically challenging problem you have solved recently?"*

---

## 🎯 Tips for Meta Behavioral Loops
1.  **De-emphasize Red Tape**: Meta values engineers who can navigate ambiguity and build without waiting for long approval cycles. Emphasize how you took initiative.
2.  **Focus on Metrics**: Meta measures success through data. Be prepared to explain exactly how you measured your project wins.
3.  **Demonstrate Simplicity**: Avoid over-engineering. Highlight cases where you built a simple, scalable solution that solved the problem without adding architectural weight.
