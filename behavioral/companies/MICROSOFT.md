# 🏢 Company Profile: Microsoft

Microsoft's culture is centered around a **Growth Mindset** and **collaboration**. They value "learn-it-alls" over "know-it-alls" and look for engineers who make others better.

---

## 💎 Microsoft Core Values

### 1. Growth Mindset
*   **Signal**: Embracing challenges, learning from failures, seeking feedback proactively, and adjusting behavior.
*   **Key Question**: *"Tell me about a time you received critical feedback that changed how you approach your work."*

### 2. Collaboration & Inclusion
*   **Signal**: Helping others succeed, collaborating across boundaries, and building on the ideas of others.
*   **Key Question**: *"Tell me about a project where you built on another team's platform or technology instead of building your own."*

### 3. Customer Focus & Empathy
*   **Signal**: Understanding user pain points, obsessing over accessibility and customer satisfaction.
*   **Key Question**: *"How do you gather feedback from customers or users to guide your engineering decisions?"*

---

## 🎯 Tips for Microsoft Behavioral Loops
1.  **Show Humility**: Do not be afraid to talk about failure (S03). Focus on what you learned and how you grew from the experience.
2.  **Highlight Team Win**: Emphasize how you shared knowledge, helped junior devs (S12), and contributed to a collaborative environment.
3.  **Customer Empathy**: Show that you understand the real-world impact of your code on end-users.
