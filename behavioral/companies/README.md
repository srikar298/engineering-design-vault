# 🏢 Zone 4 — Companies

Before you enter any interview loop, you must conduct deep, targeted research on the target company. Use this folder to compile company-specific intelligence, culture principles, and tailored interview scripts.

---

## 🏗️ The Company Research Checklist

For every company you interview with, fill out the following checklist:
*   [ ] **Mission Statement**: What is their stated core business mission?
*   **Key Products**: What are their top 3 revenue-driving products or services?
*   **Recent News**: What is the most significant announcement or tech-blog post from the last 3 months?
*   **Tech Stack**: What are their primary languages, databases, and infrastructure tools?
*   **Salary Bounds**: What is the levels.fyi compensation band for your target level?

## 🗣️ Stand-out Questions to Ask the Interviewer
*Always have 3-4 highly tailored questions ready at the end of the loop.*

### Technical Questions
*   *"What is the biggest technical bottleneck or system bottleneck your team is currently working on?"*
*   *"How does the team balance shipping fast features with managing technical debt?"*
*   *"What does the typical on-call rotation look like? How does the team run incidents?"*

### Culture Questions
*   *"What is something about the engineering culture here that surprised you after you joined?"*
*   *"How does the team resolve disagreements about architectural directions?"*
*   *"What does career progression look like for individual contributors (ICs) at my level?"*
