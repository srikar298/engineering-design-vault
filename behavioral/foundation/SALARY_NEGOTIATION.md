# 💼 Salary & Negotiation Script

Negotiation starts during the first call. Use these scripts to remain collaborative, protect your value, and avoid anchoring yourself to a low number prematurely.

---

## 💬 1. Handling the Recruiter Screen
*When the recruiter asks: "What are your salary expectations?" or "What is your current compensation?"*

### ⚠️ Rules:
1.  **Do not mention a number first.**
2.  **Redirect focus to the role, scope, and total value.**
3.  **Ask for their budgeted range.**

### 📝 Redirect Script:
> *"I'm looking for a competitive offer that aligns with market rates for this role and level at `[Company]`. Since compensation depends on the complete package — including equity, bonuses, and benefits — I'm flexible. What is the budgeted salary and equity range you have allocated for this position?"*

## ⚔️ 3. Handling the Final Offer (Increase Request)
*When you receive an offer but want to negotiate up:*

### 📝 Negotiation Script:
> *"Thank you so much for the offer. I am incredibly excited about joining the team and working on `[Project]`. Based on my current compensation discussions and the market value for this level of ownership, I was hoping we could get closer to `[Target Base/Equity]`. If we can bridge that gap, I am ready to sign the offer today."*
