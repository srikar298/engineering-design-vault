## 🔍 2. Pattern Analysis & Diagnosis

### Conflict Story Check
*   **Observed Pattern**: Do your conflict stories (S04, S10) receive low grades (below 3.5)?
*   **Common Cause**: Lack of specific resolution details or failing to show empathy for the other party.
*   **Fix Action**: Re-draft the story highlighting what *exactly* you said, how you listened to their inputs, and how the system benefited.

### Metric Density check
*   **Observed Pattern**: Are you hitting the 2-minute mark but failing to convince the interviewer?
*   **Common Cause**: Missing specific system parameters (QPS, CPU, latencies).
*   **Fix Action**: Audit stories S01, S02, S08, and S11. Add "approximately" or range metrics to the Result (R) block.

### Filler Word Density
*   **Observed Pattern**: Spoken answers contain > 4 filler words per minute.
*   **Common Cause**: Speaking before thinking or rushing to avoid silence.
*   **Fix Action**: Use the 3-second strategic pause before answering. Video record yourself.
