# 🤝 Peer Mock Schedule

Live mocks with real humans are non-negotiable. A peer asking an unexpected follow-up question teaches you more than 2 weeks of solo writing.

> **Start mocking at Week 1. Not Week 7.**
> Even a rough, incomplete mock in Week 1 reveals gaps that 30 hours of writing won't surface.

---

## 🏗️ Mock Scheduling Framework

### Who Can Be Your Mock Partner?
*   **Best**: Another engineer preparing for interviews (mutual, no cost)
*   **Good**: Any engineer friend, ex-colleague, or senior developer
*   **Decent**: Pramp.com (free peer matching platform), interviewing.io (paid)
*   **Minimum viable**: AI assistant asking questions while you record yourself

### Ideal Cadence by Phase:

| Week | Mock Type | Duration | Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Week 1** | Self-recording | 20 min | Raw baseline — see where you are |
| **Week 2** | Peer (informal) | 30 min | 3 questions + rough feedback |
| **Week 3** | Peer | 40 min | 4 questions, use MOCK_SIMULATOR scoring |
| **Week 4** | Peer (company-specific) | 45 min | Simulate Amazon LP round |
| **Week 5** | Peer (blind) | 45 min | Partner picks questions. You don't know them. |
| **Week 6** | Pramp / interviewing.io | 45 min | Stranger asks — highest signal |
| **Week 7** | Full loop simulation | 60 min | TMY + 4 Qs + their questions |
| **Week 8** | Repeat Week 7 x 2 | 60 min each | Pre-interview dress rehearsal |

## 📋 Mock Feedback Template (Give This to Your Partner)

*Copy-paste this and send to whoever is mocking you:*

```
Hi [Name], thanks for mocking with me! Could you grade me on these 5 things?

After each of my answers, please note:
1. STAR completeness (1-5): Did I cover Situation, Task, Action, Result clearly?
2. Specificity (Y/N): Did I say a specific metric or exact step, or was I vague?
3. "I" vs "We" (Y/N): Did I speak to my specific contribution vs. the team's?
4. Energy & Confidence (1-5): Did I sound certain and engaged, or hesitant?
5. One follow-up question you'd want to ask (please actually ask it)

At the end: What's my single biggest gap?
```

## 🚦 Mock Readiness Gates

Use these gates to decide when you are ready to apply to a company:

| Gate | Criteria | Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gate 1** | Completed at least 1 self-recording session per story | `[ ]` |
| **Gate 2** | Completed 2 peer mocks with scored feedback | `[ ]` |
| **Gate 3** | Average mock score ≥ 3.5 across last 3 sessions | `[ ]` |
| **Gate 4** | Completed 1 company-specific mock (Amazon or Google) | `[ ]` |
| **Gate 5** | 0 stories rated below 3.0 in MOCK_REVIEW_LOG | `[ ]` |
| **🟢 Apply** | All 5 gates passed | `[ ]` |
