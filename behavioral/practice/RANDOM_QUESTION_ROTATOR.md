# 🎲 Random Question Rotator

Pre-shuffled question batches so you never practice in predictable order.  
**Rule**: Never do Q01 → Q02 → Q03 in sequence. Interviewers are random. Your muscle memory must be too.

---

## How to Use
1. Find your current week below.
2. Each day has one **Primary** question and one **Wildcard** follow-up.
3. Answer the Primary. If time allows, also answer the Wildcard.
4. Cross off questions you've practiced. When a week is done, rotate to next week.

> **Repeat weeks 1-4 after completing week 8. Spaced repetition is the method.**
