# 🎭 The 12-Story Bank

Welcome to the Story Bank. You do not need dozens of stories to pass a senior loop; you need **12 exceptional stories** that are metric-dense, true, and highly adaptable.

---

## 🏗️ STAR+ Structural Rules

Every story in this index must be rehearsed spoken-out-loud to fit within **2 minutes (120 seconds)**. Keep your content balanced:

*   **[S] Situation (~15s)**: Set the context. Trailing QPS, scale of data, legacy monolith, DB engine.
*   **[T] Task (~15s)**: Detail *your* specific technical responsibility.
*   **[A] Action (~75s)**: Explain *what* you did and *why* you did it. Highlight trade-offs considered.
*   **[R] Result (~20s)**: Quantified outcomes (e.g., Latency: 800ms -> 120ms).
*   **[+] Reflection (~10s)**: What did you learn or what would you change today?
