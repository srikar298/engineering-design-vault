## ⏱️ Practice Guidelines for SDE-2 Candidates
1. **Prioritize the '🔥 Mandatory' Problems**: If you are short on time, ensure you solve all 24 labeled SDE-2 core sheets first.
2. **Time Box**: Allocate exactly 45 minutes for whiteboarding and drawing layout diagrams.
3. **Identify Bottlenecks**: Dig deep into hot shards, caching strategies, and data consistency models.
4. **Post-mortem Notes**: Document your specific mistakes and feedback inside the problem's study notes sheet.
