# 🎯 Machine Coding Roadmap: SDE-1 to SDE-4

> **"A Junior Developer implements the requirements. A Senior Engineer designs for the scale, concurrency, and extensibility of 2025."**

This roadmap is categorized by difficulty. For each level, master the **Invariants** and **Concurrency** before moving up.

---

## 🟢 Level 1: Foundations (SDE-1 / Junior)
*Focus: OOP Pillars, SOLID, and Simple State.*

1.  **Tic-Tac-Toe** (Grid logic)
2.  **Snake and Ladder** (Turn management)
3.  **Tetris / 2048** (Coordinate manipulation)
4.  **Minesweeper** (Recursive reveal)
5.  **Library Management** (Resource relationships)
6.  **Parking Lot (Basic)** (Entity modeling)

---

## 🟡 Level 2: Intermediate (SDE-2 / Professional)
*Focus: Design Patterns, Thread-Safety, and Workflows.*

1.  **Movie Ticket Booking (BookMyShow)** (Concurrency & Double-booking)
2.  **Internet Download Manager (IDM)** (Multithreaded byte-range requests)
3.  **Zepto Coupon System** (Pluggable rules/Strategy)
4.  **Rate Limiter** (Concurrency & Token Bucket)
5.  **Vending Machine / ATM** (State Pattern & CoR)
6.  **Elevator System** (Scheduling Algorithms)
7.  **OYO / Airbnb** (Inventory & Booking state)
8.  **Thread Pool** (Queue & Worker lifecycle)
9.  **Google Calendar** (Date-range conflict logic)
10. **Logging Library (Log4j)** (Singleton & Decorator)
11. **JSON Parser** (Composite Pattern)

---

## 🟠 Level 3: Advanced (SDE-2+ / SDE-3 / Senior)
*Focus: Infrastructure, Distributed Invariants, & Peer-to-Peer.*

1.  **AWS S3 Service** (Blob metadata & Chunking)
2.  **Google Drive** (Version control & File sync)
3.  **GitHub Internals** (Git objects: Blobs, Trees, Commits)
4.  **Tinder Dating App** (Geospatial matching & Feed Strategy)
5.  **WhatsApp / Slack** (Real-time Messaging & Backpressure)
6.  **Google Docs** (Collaborative editing: CRDT/OT)
7.  **Crypto Exchange** (Order Book & Matching Engine)
8.  **Payment Recommendation** (Ranking Strategy)
9.  **Video Conferencing (Zoom)** (WebRTC Signaling logic)
10. **Chess Game** (Complex move validation & Undo/Redo)

---

## 🔴 Level 4: Architect / Founding Engineer (SDE-4)
*Focus: Agentic AI, Distributed Systems logic, & Performance Engines.*

1.  **AI Agentic Workflow** (Task planning & Tool execution)
2.  **Vector Database Indexer** (Embedding & Semantic Hashing)
3.  **Game Engine (Unreal Lite)** (System decoupling: Physics/Logic/Render)
4.  **Distributed L1/L2 Cache** (Consistency models)
5.  **Alexa / Voice Assistant** (Intent mapping & Pipeline orchestration)
6.  **Service Discovery Sidecar** (Health & Connectivity logic)

---

## 📈 Learning Path
1.  **Start Level 1:** Get it compilable in < 25 mins.
2.  **Master Level 2:** Implement thread-safety and 2 design patterns in 35 mins.
3.  **Crush Level 3:** Explain the trade-offs of distributed state in 45 mins.
4.  **Innovate Level 4:** Lead the discussion on AI collaboration and low-level performance.
